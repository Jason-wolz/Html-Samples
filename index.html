Final Fantasy V

The Game

Unravel the mystery of the crystals! Explore the world as you fight for the sake of it's very existence. Stop the rise of the warlock Exdeath before he throws the world into the magical equivalent of a black hole. Play the following heroes:

  • The wandering warrior Bartz and his chocobo companion
  • Princess Lenna of Tycoon, in search of her father
  • The pirate captain Faris
  • And the amnesiac Galuf.

Gameplay

Every moment counts in this game, where you can find yourself wiped out in seconds. With the ATB guage in play, there are no breaks until you vanquish your foes.
